Post Malone Hollywood's Bleeding

Description

Hollywood's Bleeding is the third studio album by American rapper and singer Post Malone. It was released on September 6, 2019, by Republic Records. This album won the 2019 American Music Awards' Favorite Album-Rap/Hip Hop. The tracklist is listed below.
