Travis Scott: Astroworld

Description

Astroworld is the third studio album by American rapper and record producer Travis Scott, released on August 3, 2018, through Cactus Jack Records and Grand Hustle Records, and distributed by Epic Records. This album was a Top 10 album on the US Billboards in 2018 and 2019, and went 3x platinum. The tracklist is listed below.
