About Album

Here at Album, we're your number outlet for vinyls, new school and classic.

We strive to satisfy your musical needs. Our family at Album strives to make sure each each customer leaves with the vinyl(s) that is perfect for them. We have a wide selection of vinyls of different genres and eras to deliver with our outstanding cutomer service.

The founder of our Album family decided to make his idea a reality in 2020. Music played a big role in his life. Music was something that allowed him to connect to people. Whether sad music played during rough times or upbeat music was played during a time of celebration, music brough people together. Music was always part of our founder's life. Because held music so close to his heart, our founder hoped music could impact others the way it impacted him. By founding Album, he hopes the music can bring people together and preserve one of his favorite forms of music, the vinyl.

Album serves customers from all regions of the world. Along with the goal to preserve vinyls, Album is also an eco-friendly. We try to get you your products using al little paper or boxes as possible. We hope you enjoy the products as much as our Album family does. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us anytime, and we will try to respond ASAP!
