Return Policy

Album accepts returns. A return will be honored if the product is unopened in the original packaging. A receipt or proof of purchase will be required. During the return process, Album will examine the product. Album will contact you via e-mail regarding the status of the return. If processed, a refund will be provided in your preference of the original form of payment or store credit. Only regular priced items may be returned. Items bought on sale are final sales.
